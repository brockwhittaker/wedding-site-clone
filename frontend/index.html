FAQ

What is the attire for the wedding?

Attire is black-tie optional (suits for men, cocktail dresses or gowns for women).

What is the weather like in Newport?

In early September the weather is usually amazing! Mid 70s during the day and 60s in the evening.

How best do I travel to Newport?

The closest airport is Providence (PVD) which is about 30 minutes from Newport. Alternatively, Boston (BOS) has more flights, but you'll need to drive 90 minutes to get into town.
